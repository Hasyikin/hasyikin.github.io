Audi's Model

The A3 Sedan

SPORTY AND PRACTICAL

The Audi A3 Sedan combines coupé characteristics with elegant design. Muscular taut surfaces and strongly flared mudguards with pronounced wheel arches frame the wheels and ensure a sporty appearance. The innovative technology, including the Audi virtual cockpit, contributes to a comfortable driving experience.

The Audi A5 Sportback

ELEGANT AND VERSATILE, CLASSIC YET ADVANCED.

Athletics and elegance combined in functionality: The Audi A5 Sportback offers sporty avant-garde coupled with the outstanding interior space offered by a sedan. State of the art technologies and innovative assistance systems provide safety for the essentials: allowing you to concentrate on having fun while you drive.

The Audi A7 Sportback

A true design masterpiece, the Audi A7 Sportback melds classic automotive elegance with its own distinctive contemporary edge. Captivating from any angle, the A7 Sportback draws you in from the first glance with its sweeping, coupe-like roofline, the interplay between taut surfaces and the flowing, sculpted line.

The Audi Q2

A REAL CHARACTER

Whether on an off-road adventure or for everyday driving in a big city – the new Audi Q2 is the all-rounder. The new Audi Q2 takes to the stage - an urban type with sharp edges, a unique character and an entirely new geometrical design vocabulary. Its furnishing leaves nothing to be desired, and consists of features that were previously reserved for the luxury class. And the connectivity? Exemplary, with the latest generation of infotainment and sound systems.

The Audi Q5

Don't leave anything to chance. Whether it comes to comfort, style, or dynamics, the Audi Q5 will win you over from the first moment. Expressive appearance, powerful drive, and pioneering technology. Your options are just as diverse. Every day anew – with the Audi Q5.

The Audi Q7

THE EPITOME OF QUATTRO

The Audi Q7 is the result of an ambitious idea: it never ceases to improve. It is masculine, yet exudes lightness. It offers comfort at the highest level.

The Audi Q8

Expressive design, pioneering technology and luxuriously appointed – the new Audi Q8 combines everything the brand stands for. Strong contours and athletically tight surfaces convey a feeling of power, sophistication and the dynamics of the quattro all-wheel drive. Inside, the styling is elegant and clean, finished with the highest quality materials. Intelligent operating systems and high-tech infotainment lead into the digital age, from MMI touch response to voice control with natural speech recognition.
